INCIDENT / NEAR-MISS REPORT

Report Number: INC-2023-004
Date of Incident: 2023-03-12
Time: 10:45 hours
Classification: NEAR-MISS
Severity: MEDIUM

LOCATION
--------
Area: Crude Distillation Unit (CDU)
Specific Location: P-101A pump skid, ground floor

PERSONNEL INVOLVED
------------------
Discovered By: Mohammed Farooq (EMP-003), Process Operator
Reported To: Sunita Patel (EMP-004), Shift Supervisor

INCIDENT DESCRIPTION
--------------------
During routine hourly rounds, Operator Mohammed Farooq detected hydrocarbon odor near the P-101A pump area. Visual inspection revealed minor dripping from the mechanical seal area. Estimated leak rate was approximately 0.1 liters/hour.

Immediate Actions Taken:
1. Operator informed Shift Supervisor immediately
2. Area isolated with warning signs
3. Portable gas detector deployed - readings below LEL
4. Maintenance contacted for emergency repair
5. Operations prepared to switch to standby pump P-101B

IMMEDIATE CAUSE
---------------
Mechanical seal leak due to premature wear of seal faces.

UNDERLYING FACTORS
------------------
1. Seal failure occurred only 2 months after preventive maintenance (WO-2023-001)
2. During PM, lubricant was changed to a new brand (EconoLube Standard 320)
3. Possible correlation between lubricant change and seal degradation - requires investigation

POTENTIAL CONSEQUENCES (IF UNDETECTED)
--------------------------------------
- Hydrocarbon accumulation in confined area
- Potential fire/explosion if ignition source present
- Environmental release to atmosphere

CORRECTIVE ACTIONS
------------------
Immediate:
- Seal replaced under WO-2023-003
- Pump returned to service

Recommended:
- Investigate potential lubricant-seal compatibility issue
- Review PM procedures for lubricant specifications

LESSONS LEARNED
---------------
1. Regular operator rounds are critical for early leak detection
2. Changes to maintenance materials (lubricants) should be reviewed for equipment compatibility
3. Seal performance should be monitored closely after any PM activity
