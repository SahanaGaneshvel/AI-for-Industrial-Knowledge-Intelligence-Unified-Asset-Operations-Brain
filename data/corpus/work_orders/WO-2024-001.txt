- Lubricant sample from bearing housing sent to external lab for analysis
- Lab Report #LAB-2024-0142 pending - results expected in 2 weeks

Preliminary Assessment: Elastomer degradation suggests possible chemical incompatibility. Root cause under investigation. Maintenance engineering to review recent service history.
