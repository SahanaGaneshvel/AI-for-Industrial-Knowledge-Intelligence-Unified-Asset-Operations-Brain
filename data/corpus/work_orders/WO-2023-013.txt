VANTARA PETROCHEM - UNIT 3
MAINTENANCE WORK ORDER

Work Order Number: WO-2023-013
Date Issued: 2023-09-01
Priority: CRITICAL
Status: CLOSED

EQUIPMENT INFORMATION
---------------------
Tag Number: P-101A
Equipment Type: Centrifugal Pump
Description: Primary feed pump for crude distillation unit
Location: CDU Area, Ground Floor
Criticality: HIGH

WORK DESCRIPTION
----------------
Type: Emergency Corrective Maintenance
Problem/Reason: Significant seal leak detected - hydrocarbon dripping at rate requiring immediate intervention. Operations reported smell of hydrocarbon in area.

ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
-------------------
Finding: Mechanical seal complete failure
Analysis: Seal faces severely damaged. Elastomer components (O-rings and secondary seals) showed signs of degradation - swelling and loss of elasticity observed. Failed seal preserved for further analysis.

Root cause: Under investigation. Urgency to restore pump prevented detailed analysis.

WORK PERFORMED
--------------
Technician: Priya Sharma (EMP-002)
Date Completed: 2023-09-01
Downtime Hours: 12

Actions Taken:
1. Emergency shutdown of P-101A per ESD procedure
2. Area cordoned off, gas testing performed - LEL 15% initially
3. Fire watch stationed, area ventilated
4. LOTO applied once LEL below 5%
5. Removed failed mechanical seal
6. Documented seal condition with photographs
7. Cleaned seal chamber thoroughly
8. Installed new mechanical seal
9. Refilled bearing housing with existing lubricant stock (EconoLube Standard 320)
10. Startup and monitoring for 2 hours - no leaks

Parts Used:
- Mechanical seal cartridge John Crane 4620 (1) - Stock Code SEAL-P101-001
- EconoLube Standard 320 lubricant oil (2 L) - Stock Code LUB-003

NOTES
-----
This is the second seal failure on P-101A in 6 months (previous failure WO-2023-003 in March). Technician recommends maintenance engineering review this pump's recent history. Seal preserved for vendor analysis.

SAFETY OBSERVATIONS
-------------------
Near-miss reported separately (INC-2023-008). Leak detected during day shift with full manning. If leak had occurred at night with reduced staffing, potential for escalation was significant.
